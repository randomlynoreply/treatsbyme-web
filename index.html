Common Questions

How far in advance should I order? +

At least 2–3 weeks for most cakes. For large wedding or sculpted cakes, 4–6 weeks is recommended. Popular dates (school holidays, long weekends) fill up fast — enquire early!

Do you cater to dietary requirements? +

Yes — please mention any dietary needs (gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, halal) in your enquiry and we'll do our best to accommodate. Some options may affect pricing.

Do you deliver? +

Currently cakes are available for pickup in Sydney. Delivery may be available for large tiered cakes — please mention this in your enquiry.

What is the deposit & payment process? +

A 50% deposit is required to confirm your booking. The remaining balance is due 1 week before your event date. We'll discuss payment details during your consultation.

About the Baker

Hi, I'm the baker
behind Treats By Me

What started as a hobby quickly became something I couldn't stop thinking about. There's something magical about turning flour, butter, and a brief conversation into a cake that makes someone's eyes light up.

Every cake at Treats By Me is made by hand — just me, in my kitchen in Sydney, pouring care into every layer, every petal, every detail. I genuinely love what I do, and I think you can taste it.

Whether you have a crystal-clear vision or just a vague "pink and pretty" idea, I'll help you bring it to life. That's my favourite part of the job.
